Welcome to Kosovo Football

Kosovo football has a rich and inspiring history. The Football Federation of Kosovo (FFK) is responsible for developing the sport in the country. Since joining FIFA and UEFA in 2016, the national team has rapidly risen in prominence.

National Football Team

Nicknamed the "Dardanians," the Kosovo national team has impressed fans worldwide with competitive performances in international tournaments. Key players include:

  • Milot Rashica - Winger known for his pace and creativity.
  • Amir Rrahmani - Captain and defensive leader.
  • Vedat Muriqi - Prolific striker with a powerful aerial presence.

The team’s passionate performances showcase Kosovo's fighting spirit and determination.

Domestic Competitions

The "Superliga e Futbollit të Kosovës" is Kosovo's top football league. Prominent clubs include:

  • FC Drita - Consistent title contenders.
  • KF Prishtina - One of the oldest and most successful clubs.
  • KF Ballkani - Rising team with recent successes.
